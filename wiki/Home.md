## ğŸ® How to Play

1. Click **"Start Quiz"** on the welcome screen
2. Read each question carefully (you have 15 seconds!)
3. Select your answer from 4 options
4. See immediate feedback (green for correct, red for wrong)
5. Automatically move to the next question
6. View your final score and review all answers
7. Click **"Restart Quiz"** to try again!
